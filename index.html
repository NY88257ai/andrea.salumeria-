Our Story

Andrea Salumeria has been serving the Jersey City community since 1975. What started as a small neighborhood salumeria has grown into a destination for homemade mozzarella, classic Italian sandwiches, imported cheeses, cured meats, and pantry staples from the old country.

Nearly fifty years later, the traditions remain the same: quality ingredients, generous portions, and warm, familiar faces behind the counter. Whether you’re grabbing a hero for lunch, ordering trays for a family party, or stocking your kitchen with real Italian products, we’re proud to be part of your table.
